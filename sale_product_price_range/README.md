# Sale Product Price Range

This module adds an allowed sales price range on products and shows that range
to salespeople while creating sale orders.

Included features:

- enable a sales price range on each product
- define minimum and maximum sale price on the product form
- show the allowed range on sale order lines
- show an in-range / out-of-range status to the salesperson
- highlight sale order line pricing when the entered unit price is outside the configured range
